What are Electric Vehicles?

Electric vehicles (EVs) are powered by electricity stored in batteries instead of traditional fuels like petrol or diesel. They offer a cleaner, more sustainable way to travel.

Types and Brands of Electric Vehicles

Electric vehicles come in various types, including:

  • Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs)
  • Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs)
  • Fuel Cell Electric Vehicles (FCEVs)

Some popular brands producing EVs are Tesla, Nissan, Chevrolet, Hyundai, and BMW.

Challenges Driving the Use of EVs

  • Rising fuel costs
  • Environmental concerns about pollution and carbon emissions
  • Government incentives and subsidies for green technologies
